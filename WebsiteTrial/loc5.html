Beaufort, SC

Overview

Beaufort is a city tucked into winding, historic streets framed by natural foilage and centuries of old, moss-draped oak trees.

It is the second-oldest city it South Carolina and is known for its scenic location and maintaining a historic character by preservation of its antebellum architecture.

Henry C. Chambers Waterfront Park

Henry C. Chambers Waterfront Park is a gorgeous but short retreat for an afternoon picnic or short walk. It extends along the Beaufort River with its beautiful southern and tropical landscaping. It is definently the crown jewel of the city and not something you would want to miss.
