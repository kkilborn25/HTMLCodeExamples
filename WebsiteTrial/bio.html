Doc McStuffins Biography

Childhood:

As a child, Doc really enjoyed helping and improving others health. She spent most of her time educating herself and learning new skills that kids her age had no idea about. Her curiosity typically helped her in her school. She graduated as valedictorian of her class. With many years of learning left in her lifetime, she went to college to pursue a career as a doctor. Once she recieved her degree(way earlier than most), and completed multiple years in her passion, she decided that she was set out to be more than just a doctor. She wanted to make a difference in a larger number of peoples' lives.

Family:

Doc has two younger siblings Donny and Maya McStuffins. She always enjoys spending time with her two younger siblings and her very supportive parents. They enjoy going on camping trips and enjoying the holidays in each others' company. Doc is married to her child-hood next door neighboor and best-friend, Henery. Together, the two have started a family. They have a 10 year old girl, Hallie, an 8 year old girl, Emmie, and a 5 year old boy, Carlos.

Characteristics:

Doc is very compassionate. She is willing to help anyone and anything whenever they are in trouble. She is empathetic because she always understands and demonstrates empathy to her patients and friends. She is also curious. She never wants the simple answer behind something. She wants to know how any why everything occurs the way it does. She is finally repectful and kind-hearted. She never is rude or unkind to anyone she is always positive and willing to put people before herself as well.
