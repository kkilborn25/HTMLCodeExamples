Myrtle Beach, SC

Overview

Myrtle Beach is known for its wide, white-sand beaches on the Grand Strand. There are many activities around the town as well. You can enjoy wide beaaches, warm weather, an incredible range of entertainment, nightlife, gold, shopping, dinning, and theatre.

Brookgreen Gardens

Brookgreen Gardens is known as a botanical and sculpture garden. It covers 1600 acres and includes 18th and 19th century plantation sites, old plantation rice fields, and garden areas complete with sculpture.
