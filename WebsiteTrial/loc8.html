Spartanburg, SC

Overview

Spartanburg is packed full of outdoor adventure, restaurants, and all kinds of exciting events.

The city is home to thriving cultures with public art around almost every corner.

Croft State Park

Croft State Park was once an army training base. It covers more than 7,000 acres of rolling, wooded terrain just a few miles from downtown Spartanburg.

The park offers over 20 miles of biking and hiking trails, a playground, picnicking and camping, as well as fishing and boating in one of two lakes.

BMW Zentrum

BMW Zentrum is where all of BMW comes together in a unique building. You can expect to see cars and innovation all for free. It's an ultimate experience you won't want to miss!
