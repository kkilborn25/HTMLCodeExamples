Columbia, SC

Overview

Columbia is the capital of South carolina. It is the second largest city in South Carolina as of the 2020 census. It is a creative hub fed by university and capital city communities. There is typically a very vibrant energy around the entire city.

South Carolina State House

The South Carolina State House is home to the Governor's office as well as the House and Senate chambers. Free guided and self-guided tours of the State House are available Monday-Saturday.

University of South Carolina

The University of South Carolina is more than 200 years old, yet it is a place where new ideas, solutions, and breakthroughs are born daily.
